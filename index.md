Retirement planning ensures financial security during non-working years, offering independence and preparation for healthcare costs. It empowers individuals to enjoy their golden years, minimize dependency, and strategically manage assets for a comfortable and fulfilling retirement. It is a crucial aspect of holistic financial management that considers both the present and the future.
